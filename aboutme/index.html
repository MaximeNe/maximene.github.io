Learn more about me

My name is Maxime, I’m a French FullStack engineer. I’ve always loved computer science because it’s a very fast-growing field in which we can participate actively. I then heard about artificial intelligence and I learned about it and became passionate about AI. I also love programming and I’m always looking for new things to learn.

I am currently working fulltime at @SOLUTEC on two differents projects. The first one is an employee management application, the second a asset management app. (C# .NET MVC and C# .NET BLAZOR).

In the past years, I did some research on AI, in computer vision, log analysis and NLP. You can learn more about it in the “My Work” section.

I was the vice-president of Ens’IA for two consecutive year, an association dedicated to sharing AI knowledge at my school.

During my free time, I love riding bikes (motorcycle and MTB) and doing mechanics by myself. I also apreciate filming trips to share how beautiful France is.
